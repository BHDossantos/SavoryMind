# DealFlow AI

**Vision.** A web platform that helps small-business buyers find, analyze,
score, negotiate, and track acquisitions — restaurants, gyms, bars, salons,
retail, services — by turning raw financials into a structured deal score,
risk profile, fair-value estimate, and AI-generated negotiation playbook in
seconds.

**Target users.** First-time buyers, entrepreneurs, small investors,
operator-buyers, and brokers — anyone evaluating an SMB acquisition without a
CFO on staff.

**Core promise.** Enter what you know about a deal → instantly get true profit,
EBITDA, fair value, risk flags, suggested offer, walk-away price, payback
timeline, and an opinionated AI investment thesis with a verdict and
due-diligence checklist. Saved deals flow through a Lead → Closed pipeline.
Every analysis is reproducible, exportable, and shareable.

**Why now.**

- Listings show revenue, not real profit.
- Sellers inflate numbers and omit costs.
- Buyers lack a standardized way to compare deals or know what's fair.
- Traditional SMB advisory is inconsistent and expensive.
- A rule-based engine plus Claude turns a $5K consult into a $0.05 instant
  analysis.

**Non-goals (v1).** No deal marketplace, no broker network, no real
backend/auth, no team collaboration, no payments. Single-user, browser-local
persistence. AI assist is optional (degrades gracefully without an API key).

**Non-goals (ever, unless re-scoped).** We are not a brokerage, not a
regulated financial-advice product, and not a mobile-native app.
